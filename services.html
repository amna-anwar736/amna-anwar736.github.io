Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan? +
Yes, absolutely. I work remotely with clients across Pakistan and internationally. All communication happens via WhatsApp, email, or video call — whatever is easiest for you.
How long does a WordPress website take? +
A standard 5-page business website typically takes 5–10 working days depending on how quickly content (text, images, logo) is provided. Larger sites take longer and will be discussed in the proposal.
How long does a Moodle LMS setup take? +
A basic Moodle installation with theme, a few courses, and user setup takes about 7–14 days. More complex setups with many courses and integrations take longer and are planned case by case.
Do you provide hosting? +
I don't sell hosting directly, but I guide you to the best and most affordable hosting options for your needs — including local Pakistani hosting providers. I handle all the technical setup once you have hosting.
What if I need changes after the project is done? +
All projects include a revision period. After delivery, minor changes are free for 7 days. Ongoing maintenance and updates can be arranged as a separate monthly plan.
Do you offer payment in Pakistani Rupees (PKR)? +
Yes! For Pakistani clients I accept PKR via bank transfer and Easypaisa/JazzCash. For international clients I accept payments through Wise or Payoneer.
